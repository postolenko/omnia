Personal training studio

Omnia personal training — comprehensive approach to your well-being. Together with you, we develop tailored programs to improve your physical and mental health.

Our program

We develop highly individualized programs and focus on sustainable improvement of your results.

Intro interview

We start with an extensive interview to set your goals and provide you with a tailored training plan.

Health check

We map your current lifestyle through our intake screening. We measure 13 skin folds and take physical assessments to evaluate your health state.

Nutrition guidance

We create a nutrition plan to support your training. We pay attention to supplements, gut health, detoxification.

Challenging workouts

Our coaches guide you in your training plan and focus on motivating workouts to get best out of your body.

Lifestyle change

We improve your lifestyle and work on your sleep, stress management and energy level.

Recovery and rehabilitation

Relax in our wellness area and promote recovery with sports massage, fascia stretching and physiotherapy.
